How It Works

  • Submit your Executive Summary (3-7 pages)
  • Our team reviews it for legitimacy and alignment
  • If accepted, we send it to SGM for review
  • If approved, we schedule a 1-hour briefing call - CEO only
  • After the briefing, you decide to proceed or withdraw
  • If proceeding, send the $600 fee via Zelle, Venmo, or Certified Check

  • If you don't get funded or choose to withdraw - your $600 is refunded
